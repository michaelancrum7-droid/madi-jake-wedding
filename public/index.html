About the Venue

Nestled in the Allegheny Mountains, The Homestead is a historic luxury property that has been welcoming guests since 1766. With stunning mountain views, historic charm, and world-class amenities, it's the perfect backdrop for Madi & Jake's special day.

From championship golf and warm mineral pools to the grand ballroom and manicured gardens, The Homestead offers an unmatched setting for an unforgettable celebration.
